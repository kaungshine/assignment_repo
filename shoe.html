About

Hailed by the German brand as "The Greatest Running Shoe Ever", the adidas UltraBOOST has been one of the top choices not only by runners, but also by style icons and sneakerheads for being comfortable and easy to pair with.

The sneaker was one of the first to be tested using the ARAMIS system which is used by top engineering institutions namely NASA, Boeing, and Audi to measure vibration, design, and ergonomics. The technology, which was used during the development of other adidas models such as the PureBOOST X and AlphaBOUNCE, uses a system of cameras and sensors to envision the movement of the runner with the sneaker.

Since its launch back in January 2015, the popular runner has been evolving through the use of different color motifs. Let's take a look at 28 of the most popular colorways that paved the name of "The Greatest Running Shoe Ever".
